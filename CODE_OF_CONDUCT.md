# Code of Conduct

SupaVector expects respectful, technically serious, and inclusive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in disagreement.
- Critique code, ideas, and behavior, not people.
- Keep reviews specific, actionable, and scoped to the change.
- Assume good intent first, then ask for clarification.
- Respect maintainer and contributor time. Stay on topic and avoid spam, harassment, or repeated low-signal disruption.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, insults, or bad-faith pile-ons
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated off-topic disruption in issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Pressuring maintainers for unpaid support or immediate response times

## Scope

This policy applies in the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, discussions, docs, and any project spaces linked from this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, and pull requests that violate this policy. They may also reject contributions or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately through the repository host instead of starting a public moderation thread.

## Project Standard

The collaboration bar for this project is clarity, good faith, technical rigor, and respect for contributors at different experience levels.
